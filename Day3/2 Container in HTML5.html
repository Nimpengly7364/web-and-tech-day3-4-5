The impact of Social media

Author: October Jensen

Social media is a big part of our daily lives, changing how we talk, share, and connect. It has many benefits, like improving communication and allowing self-expression, but also comes with problems like privacy issues, online bullying, misinformation, and too much screen time affecting mental health. It's important to use social media carefully, be aware of the risks, and balance online and real-life interactions. The impact of social media depends on our choices, so practicing responsible use is essential.
